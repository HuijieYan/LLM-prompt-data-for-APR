### Bug Explanation
The bug occurs in the `_preprocess_numpy_input` function when the `mode` is not `'tf'` or `'torch'`. However, the bug arises due to incorrect normalization and mean subtraction calculations for the color channels.

### Bug location
The bug is located in the section where the color channels are zero-centered by subtracting the mean values. The incorrect calculations lead to the image data being transformed incorrectly, causing the failing tests.
