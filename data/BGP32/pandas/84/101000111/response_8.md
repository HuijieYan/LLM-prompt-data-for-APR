### Analyze the Buggy Function

The buggy function `_unstack_multiple` is intended to unstack or reshape data based on multiple indexes. It operates on a DataFrame or a Series object and performs the unstacking operation to return a new DataFrame. 

### Potential Error Locations

1. The `clocs` processing can result in incorrect indexing.
2. Handling of hierarchical columns is not implemented yet.
3. The creation and handling of the `dummy_index` and `dummy` might contain errors.

### Cause of the Bug

The bug occurs due to how the levels in the columns of the index are handled. The error in dealing with the `clocs` leads to incorrect indexing and column reshaping, resulting in the "KeyError" later on.

### Strategy for Fixing the Bug

To fix the bug:
1. Ensure that the index levels and columns are properly processed.
2. Correct the generation of the `dummy_index` and its association with `dummy`.
3. Double-check the handling of hierarchical columns for correct unstacking.
