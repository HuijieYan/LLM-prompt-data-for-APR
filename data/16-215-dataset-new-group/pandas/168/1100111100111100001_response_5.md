The goal is to enable local references to axis, level, sort, while the passed-in axis, level, and sort are 'global'. However, the issue arises during the processing of the `key` passed to the function, causing a `KeyError`.

### Bug
The bug occurs due to the mishandling of the `key` parameter when it's a single string or a list.

### Fix
The bug can be fixed by ensuring that the processing is performed correctly based on the type of the `key` parameter, specifically when it's a single string or a list.
