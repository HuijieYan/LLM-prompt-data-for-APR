Then, it checks if the data falls within a specific range of acceptable timestamps, but this logic is unnecessary for boolean values. Finally, it attempts to convert the data to datetime using various time units, including seconds, milliseconds, microseconds, and nanoseconds. This unnecessarily complex and erroneous logic causes the bug.
