The error in the test is caused by the `pivot_table` function calling itself recursively within a loop that handles a list of `aggfunc`. This results in an infinite loop which ultimately results in a `DataFrame` being converted into a `Series`. The subsequent use of the `Series` to perform methods and operations that are meant for `DataFrame`s causes the `AttributeError`.

To fix the bug:
- Avoid the recursive call to `pivot_table` when `aggfunc` contains a list of functions.
- Instead, iterate over the list of functions in a regular loop, not making a recursive call.
