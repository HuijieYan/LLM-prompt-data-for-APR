### Analysis:
The issue is related to a problem with the `groupby` function on a specific day due to an ambiguous timestamp. The `groupby` call is raising an AmbiguousTimeError. The buggy function is `_get_time_bins` in the `pandas/core/resample.py` file.

### Error Locations:
1. The construction of the `labels` and `binner` using `date_range` does not handle ambiguous timestamps.
2. The adjustment of labels and bins based on the relative frequencies is problematic.

### Cause of the Bug:
The `date_range` function does not handle ambiguous timestamps properly, which leads to the AmbiguousTimeError when using `groupby` on a day with an ambiguous timestamp.

### Strategy for Fixing the Bug:
1. Use a different method to construct `labels` and `binner` that handles ambiguous timestamps correctly.
2. Improve the adjustment of labels and bins to accommodate relative frequencies without causing errors.
