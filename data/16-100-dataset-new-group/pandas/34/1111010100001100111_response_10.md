## Bug Explanation
The bug causes the `groupby` operation to fail with an `AmbiguousTimeError` on clock change day in Cuba, where midnight local time is an ambiguous timestamp. The issue arises when the call to `groupby` raises an error, preventing the expected output of three groups (one for each day) due to the handling of ambiguous timestamps.

The cause of the bug is that the current implementation of `_get_time_bins` does not handle the ambiguous timestamps present on clock change days. This leads to the incorrect binning of the timestamps and results in the `AmbiguousTimeError`.

## Fix Strategy
To fix the bug, the `_get_time_bins` function needs to be modified to handle ambiguous timestamps caused by clock change days in Cuba. This can be achieved by adjusting the logic for creating the time bins and labels to accommodate the ambiguous timestamps.
