### Bug Explanation
The bug is related to the handling of ambiguous timestamps during a clock change day in Cuba. A call to `groupby` raises an `AmbiguousTimeError`. This occurs because the function does not handle the ambiguous timestamp correctly.

### Bug Fix Strategy
To fix the bug, we need to update the `_get_time_bins` function to handle the ambiguous timestamp caused by the clock change day in Cuba. This can be achieved by adjusting the `start` and `end` timestamps of the date range to ensure correct labeling and grouping of the data.
