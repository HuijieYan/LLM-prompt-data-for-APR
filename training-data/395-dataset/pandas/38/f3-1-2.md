Problem description
The reason semms to mistyping in https://github.com/pandas-dev/pandas/blob/master/pandas/core/reshape/reshape.py#L366 :

clocs = [v if i > v else v - 1 for v in clocs]
I think it must be:

clocs = [v if val > v else v - 1 for v in clocs]
